Triggers

This microinteraction starts with a click on the clapping hands circle

Rules

There are three main rules within this microinteraction

  • 1. "Bouncing" animation of button
  • 2. Clapping hands will fill with gray color
  • 3. Confetti will come out of circle

    • Feedback

    The user will see

  • The button bounce
  • Celebratory confetti come out of the circle
  • The personal, and total number of claps from other readers

    • Loops & Modes

  • This interaction contains no loops or modes
  • Clap contributions are able to be repeated up to 50 times
